📧 One-time Gmail setup in Chrome
Do this once on Pete's laptop and the Save & Email button will open Gmail automatically every time:

1 Open Gmail in Chrome and make sure Pete is logged in.
2 Click the two-diamond icon in the address bar (far right of the URL bar).
3 A popup appears — click "Allow" next to "Open mailto links".
4 If you don't see that icon: go to Chrome menu ⚙ → Settings → Privacy and security → Site settings → Additional permissions → Protocol handlers → make sure Gmail is listed there.

After that, every time Pete clicks Save & Email, Chrome will open Gmail with the subject line and message already typed — he just needs to attach the PDF and press Send.
